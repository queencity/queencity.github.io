Who Are We?

In its 2nd year, QCB is a dance competition that focuses on showcasing and enriching others about Indian heritage through Bhangra -  a dance that comes from the roots of North India called Punjab. QCB is proudly hosted by Bearcat Bhangra, a student run organization from the University of Cincinnati.
